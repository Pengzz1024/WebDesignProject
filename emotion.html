Arrogance

Arrogance represented by the King, the little prince met him at the first planet

Arrogance means having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilties. An arrogant person will always think in his own way, he will put himself at the first position and therefore he couldn't find out others' merit.

In general, if you find a person who is arrogant, you should be known that he will easily complain others, you don't have to feel upset if you are treated bad by those people, it's their fault.

Vanity

Vanity represented by the conceited man, the little prince met him at the second planet

Vanity means excessive pride in or admiration of one's own appearance or achievements. A vain person likes exaggerate his achievements, moreover he will even lie a lot to pretend himeself is great. Those people are crazy about others' praise and admiration, they live in a fictitious dream which created by themself.

If you know a person who is vain, don't pay attention to arguing with them about whether their achievement is real or fake. They will never wake up from their virtul dream. You just need to give them a smile and keep in silence, they won't bother you

Sadness

Sadness represented by the tippler, the little prince met him at the third planet

Sadness means the condition or quality of being sad. It's not exclusive for adults, children could sometimes have sadness too. But children won't be sad for a long time, the adults do the opposite things.

If you have a friend who set himself in sadness for a long time, you should be patient with them and try to accompany with them. Though a sad person will be indifferent to others, he need help. He wants to stay alone because he feels lonely and helpless. A friend should bring him back from sorrow.

Blind

Blind represented by the businessman, the little prince met him at the fourth planet

Blind means lacking perception, awareness, or judgement rather than physical sight. A blind person don't possess the ablity of thinking. They will do things like others, they will think as others. In the most of time, they don't figure out the reason why he should do a thing, they just waste their energy and time and achieve nothing.

You don't have to take care of your blind friends, because they don't have their own idea, they will rely on you, which is actually bad for them.Sometimes they will get stucked by some wrong ideas, but they won't mentioned that, you could give them an advice if it's necessary.

Stubbornness

Stubbornness represented by the lamplighter, the little prince met him at the first planet

Stubbornness means having or showing dogged determination not to change one's attitude or position on something, especially in spite of good arguments or reaons to do so. A stubborn person will be crazy about his own thought and it will be very hard to change him. They don't care whether the thing is right or wrong, they just believed and insisted on it.

Don't bother yourself to arguing your stubborn friend, which might bring out a quarrel. All you need to do is waiting for a time when he figured our himself was wrong. A rock could only be moved slowly, isn't it?
